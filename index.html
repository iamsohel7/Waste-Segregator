WASTE SEGREGATION SIMULATOR (Version - 2.1)

How To Play:

  1. Kindly fill-up the form so that we can mail you your result.
  2. At first, on the left-hand side of the screen, you will need to select the number of items with which you would like to work.
  3. On the right-hand side of the screen, different waste items will be shown one after another.
  4. Below there are five bins named after each type of waste category- wet, dry, sanitary, E-waste, and Reject/hazardous waste. Select a bin to drop the waste item displayed above into that particular category.
  5. After completion, click on the submit button to get your score. Check your email to view your detailed scorecard.
